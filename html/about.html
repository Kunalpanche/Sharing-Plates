About SharingPlates

At SharingPlates, we believe in the power of food to nourish communities and create positive change. Founded with a vision to address the twin challenges of food waste and hunger, our organization is dedicated to making a difference one meal at a time.

What Drives Us

At the heart of our mission is a deep commitment to sustainability, social responsibility, and compassion. We understand the environmental and social impact of food waste, and we are passionate about finding innovative solutions to address this pressing issue. By rescuing surplus food and redirecting it to those who need it most, we not only reduce waste but also promote a more equitable and sustainable food system.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet profound: to bridge the gap between surplus food and hungry individuals. We work tirelessly to rescue excess food from businesses, restaurants, and food retailers, ensuring that perfectly good food doesn't go to waste. By redistributing this surplus food to those in need, we aim to alleviate hunger and food insecurity in our communities.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no edible food goes to waste and no one goes to bed hungry. Through our efforts, we strive to create a more sustainable and equitable food system, fostering a sense of community and shared responsibility in addressing food insecurity.

Join Us

Join us in our mission to create a world where no edible food goes to waste and no one goes to bed hungry. Together, we can make a tangible impact on our communities and build a brighter future for all.
