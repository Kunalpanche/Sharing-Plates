Sharing plates Vision & Mission

  • Vision: Creating a Sustainable Future We envision a world where surplus food is efficiently redistributed to those in need, reducing waste and ensuring no one goes hungry.
  • Mission: Bridging the Gap Our mission is to bridge the gap between surplus food and hungry individuals by connecting businesses with excess food to charities and individuals in need.
  • Commitment to Impact We are committed to making a positive impact on our communities and the environment by alleviating hunger, promoting sustainability, and fostering a more equitable food system.
